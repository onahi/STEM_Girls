A small move can change a lot

To be a STEM GIRLS AMBASSADOR, you have to follow the following procedure

  1. Purchase a STEM Girls' Kits comprises a Customized STEM GIRLS T-shirt, Stickers, Pen and a Brochure.
    • a) Put on STEM GIRLS AMBASSADOR T-Shirt, take photographs share on Facebook or Twitter page tag friends and tag STEM GIRLS Initiative: (@stemgirlsng, @stemgirls initiative).
  2. As an ambassador of STEM GIRLS, you have to encourage girls to develop interest in STEM by talking to Girls about opportunities in fields and be a representative of STEM GIRLS in your home, school, church, mosque, office and community.
    1. a) Placed the STEM GIRLS AMBASSADOR sticker on astrtegic place such as your car, Office Desk etc that will constantly remin you and the people around of your passion for STEM Girls' education and the need to keep pushing to break barriers for girls in STEM.

Benefits

  • The photo with the most likes and comments on social media for the month receives STEM GIRLS AMBASSADOR AWARDS.
